Pollution: A Threat to Our Planet and Future

Pollution contaminates air, water, and land, harming health, wildlife, and ecosystems. Sustainable actions are key to a cleaner planet.

Tips

Recycle

Recycling reduces waste, conserves natural resources, saves energy, and decreases pollution, helping to protect the environment and fight climate change.

Walking

Walking reduces carbon emissions, decreases air pollution, and lessens traffic congestion, making it an eco-friendly and healthy choice for the planet.

Conserve Energy

Conserving energy reduces carbon emissions, decreases pollution, and preserves natural resources, helping to slow climate change and protect the environment.

Success Stories

Recycling Program

A recycling program reduces waste, conserves resources, saves energy, and minimizes pollution, promoting a cleaner and more sustainable environment.

Walking over Driving

Walking instead of driving reduces carbon emissions, decreases air pollution, and conserves fuel, making it a healthier and more eco-friendly choice.

Saving Energy

Saving energy reduces carbon emissions, conserves natural resources, and decreases pollution, helping to protect the environment and fight climate change.

Challenges

Day 1 - Recycling Challenge

- Properly sort and dispose of recyclable materials.

- Avoid single-use plastics for the whole day.

Day 2 - Walking Challenge

- Walk or bike instead of using a car/motorcycle for at least one trip today.

- If using public transport, try to reduce unnecessary trips.

Day 3 - Conserve Energy Challenge

- Turn off lights and appliances when not in use.

- Reduce air conditioner or electric fan usage by maximizing natural ventilation.
